Healthy Restaurants Map

Map Information:

This map shows various healthy restarant locations in the Los Angeles area. To change the location, press view larger map and type in the desired location. You can filter the results with the price range option and the rating system. There are also descriptions about the restaurants listed underneath the search bar once you have viewed the larger map. Additional information can be found once your perferred restaurant is selected, such as the restaurant's address, website, hour, etc. Finally, the map can be zoomed in and out or dragged to any location you want. The map can aslo be viewed in satellite mode or the normal default map form.
